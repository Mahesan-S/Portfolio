Blender

This is my Blender showcase project where I created and presented all the 3D models myself. The goal of this project was to demonstrate my skills in modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering using Blender. I designed, modeled, and optimized each asset from scratch, paying attention to clean topology, realistic proportions, and proper material setup. Through this showcase, I highlighted my understanding of the complete 3D asset creation workflow, from concept and modeling to final presentation, focusing on both artistic quality and technical precision.
